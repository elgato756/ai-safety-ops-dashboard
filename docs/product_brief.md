# Product Brief: AI Risk Intelligence Platform

## One-line summary

A human-in-the-loop Trust & Safety operations tool that aggregates emerging public signals, classifies potential AI risks, and recommends review paths for analysts.

## Problem

AI product teams face rapidly evolving misuse, policy, and regulatory risks. Signals may first appear in public forums, news, developer communities, or regulatory updates. Analysts need help separating high-signal patterns from noise without handing final decisions to automation.

## Target users

- Trust & Safety analysts
- Policy operations teams
- Abuse prevention teams
- Integrity operations teams
- Legal and regulatory operations partners

## Core workflow

1. Ingest public signals.
2. Classify potential risks.
3. Estimate severity and confidence.
4. Summarize evidence and missing context.
5. Recommend whether human review is needed.
6. Route incidents to the right team.
7. Track lifecycle through triage, escalation, mitigation, and closure.

## Human-in-the-loop principle

The system does not make enforcement, legal, account-level, or policy decisions. It supports analyst review by organizing information and surfacing possible areas of concern.
